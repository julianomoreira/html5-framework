Story of the office

Did you ever wanted to be have an office and couldn't afford? Well, Don't feel bad becaus you're not the only one. The cheap solution for someone who really need to have an office is Linux. Unless you can afford Windows Server 2003 (lots of fees), you're going to want to end up with a solution that you can afford. In my case, I spent about $0 (zero) dollars.
